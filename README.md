1.1 Brief History
Mhlanga Holdings is a startup financial services company established to provide affordable and accessible investment opportunities to South Africans. The company was founded with the goal of improving financial inclusion and helping individuals build long-term financial stability through education and investment solutions.
The organisation focuses on beginner-friendly investment opportunities, financial consulting services, and wealth management solutions tailored for low- to middle-income earners. Mhlanga Holdings aims to bridge the gap between traditional financial services and underserved communities through digital accessibility and financial literacy.
The company plans to establish a strong online presence through a modern website that promotes investment awareness and simplifies access to financial services.
